What are cookies?

  • Back to How to use this web app

Cookies are small text files that are stored on a user's device by a website. They are commonly used to remember user preferences, login information, and to track user behavior for advertising and analytics purposes.

While cookies can be useful, they also raise privacy concerns as they can be used to track a user's online activity and collect personal information. It's important for users to be aware of the types of cookies that are being used on a website and how their data is being collected and used.
